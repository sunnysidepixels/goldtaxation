Simplifying Your Tax Journey

At Gold Taxation, we understand that navigating personal taxes can be daunting. Our dedicated team is here to provide you with personalized tax solutions tailored to your unique financial situation. Whether you're self-employed, a salaried employee, or managing multiple income streams, we ensure your tax filing is accurate, timely, and maximizes your potential refunds.

Our Services Include:

Self-Employed & Sole Proprietors: Expert guidance on income reporting, deductions, and GST/HST considerations.

Salaried & Commissioned Employees: Assistance with employment expenses and maximizing allowable deductions.

Rental & Investment Income: Comprehensive reporting for rental properties, capital gains, and other investment incomes.

Foreign Income & Pensions: Specialized support for reporting foreign earnings and pension incomes.

Students & Newcomers: Tailored services to help you understand and navigate the Canadian tax system.​

Flexible Filing Options:

Online Submission: Securely upload your documents through our online portal.

Drop-Off Service: Conveniently leave your documents at our Winnipeg office.

Email Filing: Send your tax documents directly to our team via email.

In-Person Appointments: Schedule a face-to-face meeting with our tax professionals.

Why Choose Gold Taxation?

Experienced Professionals: Our team stays updated with the latest tax laws to provide accurate advice.

Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees; we offer competitive rates for all our services.

Year-Round Support: We're here to assist you beyond tax season, whenever you need us.

Client-Centric Approach: We prioritize your needs, ensuring a smooth and stress-free tax filing experience.

At Gold Taxation, we provide expert tax services for individuals, families, and businesses across Winnipeg. From personal income tax filing and CRA audit support to corporate returns and bookkeeping—we deliver accurate, stress-free solutions tailored to your needs.
